Overview

Onyin Technologies Ltd is a company that focuses on building products and solutions that are needed in the in this 21 st century. As a company we innovate dynamic top software and products by drawing Africa’s top talent and bringing important partners into our platform to provide improved financial services inclusiveness for all in Africa.

Vision

We are passionate about helping Africa’s Finance Sector to embark on digital transformation, disruptive innovations, pursue growth, understand global market dynamics and ever-changing customers.

Mission

In an evolving global finance industry, Onyin T. Ltd has a team of industry specialists and innovators driving change through our innovative software application platforms that helps businesses capture maximum market share through a fast and stress-free financial procedure. Most importantly ensuring financial inclusion for the majority of Africans

Operating Values

We are obsessed with our customers. We never stop listening to customers so we can understand both their dreams and the challenges they face -- and then get to work using technology to solve their most important problems.
